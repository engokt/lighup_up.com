What services does Lightup Electricals offer?

  • Smart Home Automation: Automating lighting, security, appliances, and other home systems for enhanced comfort and efficiency.
  • General Electrical Contracting: Providing a full range of electrical installations, repairs, and maintenance services.
  • Electrical Supply: Supplying electrical components such as wiring, lighting, electrical panels, and more.
    • How does a solar/inverter system work?
    Solar panels capture energy from the sun and convert it into electricity. Inverter systems transform this electricity into usable power for your home or business. Batteries can also be included to store energy for use during the night or power outages.
    Why should I consider switching to solar energy?
    Solar energy allows you
  • Reduce electricity bills.
  • Minimize your environmental impact by using clean, renewable energy.
  • Gain independence from the national grid.
  • Ensure uninterrupted power supply during outages with backup systems.

    What is the cost of installing a solar system?
    Costs vary depending on the size of the system, your energy needs, and whether you choose additional features like battery storage. Contact us for a consultation and custom quote.
    How long does it take to install a solar or inverter system?
    Most installations take between 2 to 5 days, depending on the system's size and complexity. We provide an estimated timeline after a site Inspection.
    What is a smart home system?
    A smart home system allows you to automate and control various aspects of your home, such as lighting, appliances, security, and climate control, remotely through your smartphone or voice-activated assistants like Alexa or Google Home.
    What smart home features do you offer?
    Our smart home solutions include
  • Automated lighting.
  • Smart security systems (CCTV, smart locks, alarms).
  • Climate control (smart thermostats, HVAC systems).
    • Do I need an internet connection for my smart home system?
    While many smart home devices require an internet connection for remote access, some can operate offline through local networks or pre-configured automation settings. We offer flexible solutions based on your preferences.
